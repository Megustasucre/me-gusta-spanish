FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your first class.

Getting Started

When can I start?

Face-to-face classes begin every Monday. Online classes can start any day — just book 48 hours in advance.

Do I need prior Spanish knowledge?

Not at all. We welcome complete beginners. Your first step is a free level assessment with Fernando — a short 15-minute conversation to understand where you're starting from.

What is the level assessment?

It's a short, relaxed conversation with Fernando — no written tests, no pressure. He'll ask a few questions to gauge your vocabulary and grammar, and place you in the right group or design the right program for you.

Can I switch to a different level?

Absolutely. If you progress faster than expected — it happens! — your teacher will move you up. Just let us know.

Courses & Schedule

How many students are in a group?

Group Immersion has a maximum of 4 students. Cultural Connection has a maximum of 8. Private and Duo are 1–2 students only.

What are the class hours?

Face-to-face group classes run Monday to Friday, 8:30 AM – 12:30 PM. Private and Duo classes have flexible scheduling. Online classes adapt to your timezone — morning, afternoon, or evening.

What if I miss a class?

Let us know at least 24 hours in advance and we'll arrange a make-up class. Same-day no-shows cannot be recovered.

Are materials included?

Yes — textbook, workbook, and notebook are included in all face-to-face programs at no extra charge.

Do you give certificates?

Yes. After completing a program you receive a certificate indicating your course, level, and hours completed. It is signed by the school director.

Payment & Booking

How do I pay?

We accept PayPal, Wise, or bank transfer for international students. Cash is also welcome on arrival in Sucre.

Do I need to pay a deposit to reserve?

For face-to-face programs we ask for a small deposit to confirm your spot. The remainder is paid on arrival. Online packs are paid in full before the first session.

What is your cancellation policy?

If you cancel more than 7 days before your start date, we refund your deposit in full. Cancellations within 7 days are non-refundable, but we can reschedule your start date once at no extra cost.

Online Classes

What platform do you use for online classes?

We use Zoom for all live sessions. You'll receive a link before each class. No account is required — just a computer or phone with a camera.

Do my online pack hours expire?

Trial Class: no expiry. Starter Pack (5h): use within 60 days of purchase. Immersion Pack (10h): use within 3 months. If you need an extension, contact us — we're flexible.

Will I have the same teacher every session?

Yes — we always assign you the same teacher. Consistency matters for learning. If you'd prefer to work with a different teacher at any point, just let us know and we'll arrange it.

Accommodation

Do you offer homestay / host family placements?

Yes. We work with selected host families in Sucre who offer room and board. Living with a local family is the most effective way to practice Spanish outside the classroom. Contact us for availability and pricing.

Can you recommend hotels or hostels?

Absolutely. Sucre has a wide range of accommodation for every budget. We're happy to suggest options near the school and help you settle in. Just ask when you contact us.
