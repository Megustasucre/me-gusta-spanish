The founders

Where It All Started

Elizabeth and Fernando began developing the Me Gusta Method in 2005. After 6 years of research, they opened the school's doors in Sucre — and haven't stopped teaching since.

Co-Founder & Lead Teacher

Co-Founder · Est. 2005

Elizabeth

Methodology Expert & Lead Teacher

Elizabeth co-founded Me Gusta Spanish alongside Fernando after 6 years of building and refining the school's signature methodology. Her approach puts real conversation at the center — lessons are structured, but never rigid.

She specializes in cultural immersion and helping students move beyond grammar anxiety into natural, confident speaking. Students consistently describe her classes as the most enjoyable Spanish learning they've ever done.

Native Bolivian Certified Teacher Cultural Immersion 20+ Years

Co-Founder · Est. 2005

Fernando

Cultural Specialist & Teacher

Fernando brings deep knowledge of Bolivian history, culture, and the rhythms of everyday Spanish into every class. He co-developed the Me Gusta Method with Elizabeth, and his energy in the classroom is legendary among students.

His classes are known for their humor, warmth, and remarkable ability to make complex grammar feel effortless. Fernando believes that understanding Bolivian culture is inseparable from learning to speak the language well.

What makes us different

Not Just Teachers.
Guides Through a Culture.

Every teacher at Me Gusta Spanish is native Bolivian — meaning you're not just learning a language, you're learning how people actually live it. Slang, humor, local expressions, cultural references.

Our team was built around a single belief: that learning a language should be one of the most enjoyable experiences of your life. That belief shapes every lesson.

Certified & trained

All teachers hold formal teaching certifications and are continuously trained in the Me Gusta Method.

Small classes, real attention

Maximum 4 students in group classes — so your teacher knows your progress, your weak points, and your goals.
