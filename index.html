Why Choose Us

"Spanish lessons should focus on the needs of each student — because everyone is individual and learns in their own way."

Founded in 2005 by Elizabeth and Fernando, Me Gusta Spanish was built on a simple belief: that great teaching puts the student first. After six years of developing their methodology, they opened the school's doors in 2011 — and haven't looked back.

We change, improve and innovate every year. Our teachers are native Bolivian speakers who bring not just language, but culture, warmth and context to every single lesson.
