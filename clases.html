Weekly Cultural Activities

Spanish goes beyond the classroom. Every week we take you into Sucre to practice real language in real life.

City Tour

Explore Sucre's historic streets, iconic viewpoints, and hidden gems — all while practicing your listening skills.

Wallyball

A local classic — volleyball meets squash. Great exercise, guaranteed laughs, and the perfect way to bond with classmates.

Cooking Class

Learn to cook traditional Bolivian dishes while practicing food vocabulary. The best part? You eat what you make.

«Charlate» Exchange

Practice with locals, swap languages, make friends — test your Spanish in a relaxed, social setting.

Beer Tour

End the week right — taste Sucre's best craft beers, socialize, and practice conversational Spanish in a friendly atmosphere.

All Levels

What Level Are You?

We welcome students at every stage. Every new student gets a free level assessment with Fernando.

A1

Beginner

No Spanish? No problem. We start from scratch with practical, everyday language from day one.

  • Basic vocabulary & greetings
  • Simple conversations
  • Present tense foundations
B1

Intermediate

You know the basics. We push you into real conversations, complex grammar, and Bolivian cultural nuance.

  • Past & future tenses
  • Extended conversations
  • Bolivian slang & culture
C1

Advanced

Near fluent? We refine your accent, expand vocabulary, and develop academic or professional-level Spanish.

  • Subjunctive & complex grammar
  • Academic & professional writing
  • Debate & critical discussion
